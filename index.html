About this game:

Allium Asylum is a byte-sized RPG that lovingly satirizes the tropes of modern indie games.

Featuring a cast of crazy characters, bullet hell battles, and an original soundtrack. Best enjoyed with no context.

Allium Asylum is divided into 4 volumes. The free demo contains the entirety of Volume 1. Volumes 2-4 will be available to play in the full game's release.
